Demographics

Concord is a city in California northeast of San Francisco. Nearby, Briones Regional Park features views of the Diablo Valley and a trail to Diablo Foothills Regional Park, with wildflowers and views of San Francisco Bay. A former coal-mining site, Black Diamond Mines Regional Preserve includes grasslands, pepper trees and lilies. The multi-use Iron Horse Regional Trail connects Concord to the city of Pleasanton. Home to 124,074 residents, the city is the 8th largest city in the San Francisco Bay Area. Making it an Ideal location for businesses and residents.
